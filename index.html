Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data safe?
Yes! All data is stored locally on your device. Nothing is sent to servers or the cloud. Your financial information never leaves your phone.
Can I export my data?
Yes, XPensa supports exporting your data in multiple formats. You can backup and restore your data anytime.
Will there be a web version?
A web version is in the roadmap. Keep an eye on the GitHub repository for announcements.
Can I contribute to the project?
Absolutely! XPensa is open source. You can contribute on GitHub by submitting issues, pull requests, or suggesting features.
What are the system requirements?
XPensa requires Android 5.0+. It's optimized for phones with minimal RAM usage and works offline.
